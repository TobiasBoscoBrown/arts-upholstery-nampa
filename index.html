Good to know

Questions, answered

Do I need an appointment for an upholstery repair?

No. Many customers stop in for a quick look and an estimate, and small jobs are often handled same day. For larger work like a full interior we will set a time once we see it.

How much does a headliner replacement cost?

It depends on the vehicle and the pillars involved, so we give you a real quote when we see the car. Reviewers consistently call our pricing fair and competitive.

Can you match my existing seat material and color?

Yes. We match leather, vinyl and cloth by grain and color so a single panel repair blends in, and we also do full color and material changes if you want a different look.

Do you only work on cars?

No. We do auto, marine and RV seating, furniture and small leather goods. If it has a seat, a cushion or a strap, bring it in.

Where are you located?

We are at 3221 Garrity Blvd in Nampa, off Garrity near Airport Road, serving Nampa, Caldwell, Meridian and the wider Treasure Valley.
